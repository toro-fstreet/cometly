# THMC Competitor Ad Research
**Date:** March 29, 2026
**Purpose:** Analyze top hard money lending competitors' positioning, messaging, and ad strategies

---

## Competitor Overview

### 1. Kiavi (formerly LendingHome) — Largest National Player
- **Website:** kiavi.com
- **Positioning:** "Leading Hard Money Lender for Real Estate Investors"
- **Key Messaging:**
  - "Close in as fast as a few days, not weeks"
  - "Digital platform automates manual tasks"
  - "Proven ARV model trusted by tens of thousands of deals"
  - Property-based underwriting (not just borrower)
- **Loan Types:** Fix & Flip, Bridge, Rental (DSCR)
- **Coverage:** 49 states + DC
- **Landing Pages:** Dedicated `/hardmoneyhome` page for paid traffic — clean, focused on speed + technology platform
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - No traditional appraisals (internal valuations)
  - Soft credit pull only
  - Tech-forward brand (online platform emphasis)
  - Volume/scale trust signals ("tens of thousands of deals")
- **Ad Angles:** Speed, technology, scale, reliability
- **What THMC Can Learn:** Kiavi leads heavily with technology. THMC's advantage is local expertise and personal service — lean into "your deal, your lender" vs. Kiavi's automated approach

### 2. Lima One Capital — Premium National Lender
- **Website:** limaone.com
- **Positioning:** "Nation's Premier Hard Money Lender"
- **Key Messaging:**
  - "7-10 business days" closing
  - Experience-tiered pricing (rewards repeat borrowers)
  - Heavy content marketing (SEO-focused educational pages)
  - "Private money" positioning over "hard money"
- **Loan Types:** Fix & Flip (13-24 mo), Bridge, New Construction, DSCR Rental, Multifamily
- **Coverage:** Nationwide
- **Landing Pages:** Deep educational content pages (e.g., `/hard-money-loans/` is a 2000+ word guide)
  - Uses SEO content to capture organic + paid traffic
  - Client portal login prominently featured
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Experience tiers (lower rates for more deals completed)
  - Vertical construction financing
  - Educational content library
- **Ad Angles:** Expertise, comprehensive loan products, experience rewards
- **What THMC Can Learn:** Lima One's content strategy drives organic traffic that supports paid campaigns. THMC has minimal educational content. Also, Lima One's experience-tier pricing rewards loyalty — THMC could offer similar repeat borrower rates

### 3. RCN Capital — Broker-Focused
- **Website:** rcncapital.com
- **Positioning:** "Direct Financing for Real Estate Investments"
- **Key Messaging:**
  - Works with mortgage brokers AND direct borrowers
  - "Wholesale and retail lending options"
  - Established 2010 (longevity trust signal)
- **Loan Types:** Fix & Flip, Bridge, DSCR Long-term, Ground-up Construction, Multifamily (5+)
- **Coverage:** Nationwide (non-owner occupied, min $115K)
- **Landing Pages:** Clean product pages with loan program breakdowns
  - Blog content positions them as thought leaders
  - Active broker program promotion
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Dual-channel (broker + direct)
  - $115K minimum loan (higher than THMC)
  - Active content marketing + podcast
- **Ad Angles:** Broker partnerships, loan versatility, established reputation
- **What THMC Can Learn:** RCN's broker channel drive significant volume. THMC's broker program should be more prominent in ads targeting brokers specifically

### 4. Easy Street Capital — Speed Leader
- **Website:** easystreetcap.com
- **Positioning:** "Hard Money & DSCR Loans for Real Estate Investors"
- **Key Messaging:**
  - "Close in as little as 48 hours"
  - "No appraisals" (on fix & flip)
  - "Up to 93% LTC and 100% rehab draws"
  - Branded products: EasyFix, EasyRent, EasyBuild
- **Loan Types:** Fix & Flip, DSCR Rental, New Construction
- **Coverage:** Nationwide
- **Landing Pages:** Dedicated product pages (easyfix, easyrent, easybuild)
  - Heavy social proof (Google reviews prominently displayed)
  - Individual loan officer names in testimonials
  - State-specific landing pages (/hard-money-loan-new-york/)
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - 48-hour close claim (industry-leading speed claim)
  - DSCR rates from 5.75%
  - Branded loan products for clarity
  - Very strong review/testimonial game
- **Ad Angles:** Speed (48hr), no appraisal, high LTC, social proof
- **What THMC Can Learn:** Easy Street's branded products (EasyFix, EasyRent) create clear differentiation. THMC could brand their products similarly. Their state-specific landing pages are excellent for geo-targeted ads

### 5. CoreVest Finance — Portfolio Lender
- **Website:** corevestfinance.com
- **Positioning:** "Lender for Real Estate Investor Loans"
- **Key Messaging:**
  - Specializes in portfolio/rental loans
  - "Attractive rates, rapid timelines, closing certainty"
  - Build-to-rent focus
- **Loan Types:** Rental Portfolio, Bridge, Lines of Credit, Build-to-Rent, Single Family Rental
- **Coverage:** Nationwide
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Portfolio lending (multiple properties under one loan)
  - Credit lines for investors
  - Build-to-rent specialization
  - Rates from 8% for rentals
- **Ad Angles:** Portfolio financing, rental investors, scale
- **What THMC Can Learn:** CoreVest targets a different segment (portfolio landlords). THMC primarily competes in fix & flip — less overlap, but their messaging about "closing certainty" resonates

### 6. New Silver Lending — Tech-Forward
- **Website:** newsilver.com
- **Positioning:** Online hard money lender
- **Key Messaging:**
  - "Fix and Flip Loans From 9%"
  - "Up to 90% LTC"
  - Instant proof of funds
- **Loan Types:** Fix & Flip, Bridge, DSCR Rental
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - All-online process
  - Instant proof of funds letters
  - Competitive published rates

## Key Competitive Insights for THMC Ad Strategy

### THMC's Competitive Advantages to Emphasize
1. **Local Milwaukee expertise** — No national competitor can match this
2. **No credit check** — Major differentiator (most competitors require FICO)
3. **Personal service** — Named team vs. automated platforms
4. **F Street Group backing** — Private lender stability
5. **1000+ projects funded** — Credibility signal

### Gaps THMC Needs to Address
1. **No published rates or terms in ads** — Competitors like Easy Street (5.75% DSCR) and New Silver (9% fix & flip) publish starting rates
2. **No speed claim in headline** — "48 hours" (Easy Street) or "Close in days" (Kiavi) are strong hooks THMC isn't using
3. **Missing product-specific landing pages** — /bridge-loans/ returns 404, suggesting ads may land on generic pages
4. **No LTC/LTV figures published** — Investors compare these numbers; "up to 93% LTC" (Easy Street) is a strong conversion driver
5. **NMLS number not displayed** — Google compliance requirement for financial ads
6. **No state-specific landing pages beyond locations** — Easy Street has `/hard-money-loan-new-york/` style pages optimized for geo keywords

### Recommended Ad Copy Angles (Based on Competitive Analysis)

**For Core Markets (WI, MI, IL) — Non-Brand:**
- Headline: "Hard Money Loans — Close in Days | Milwaukee Lender"
- Description: "No credit check. 1000+ projects funded. Local expertise + fast funding for fix & flip investors. Apply in minutes."

**For Broker Program:**
- Headline: "Broker Program — Fast Close Hard Money | The Hard Money Co."
- Description: "Partner with a direct lender your investors trust. Quick decisions, clear terms, reliable closings. Milwaukee-based, Midwest coverage."

**Brand Defense:**
- Headline: "The Hard Money Co. — Official Site | Apply Now"
- Description: "Milwaukee's trusted hard money lender. 7-10 day closings, no credit check required. Part of F Street Group. 1000+ projects funded."
